A.P.J. ABDUL KALAM - THE LEGEND


Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam lived, (15 October 1931 - 27 July 2015) was an Indian aerospace scientist and statesman who served as the 11th president of India from 2002 to 2007.

ABOUT THE LEGEND


  • A.P.J ABDUL KALAM, full name Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was born on 15th October 1931 to a Tamil Muslim family in the centre of Rameshwaram (Pamba Island), Tamil Nadu.
  • Kalam was the youngest of four brothers and one sister in his family.
  • The young boy used to sell newspapers as a kid due to his family's financial issues.
  • He completed his early days education from Schwartz Higher Secondary School in Rameshwaram.
  • He then went to St. Joseph's college in Tiruchipalli where he completed his graduation in Physics in 1954.
  • Kalam then moved to Madras in 1955 to study Aerospace Engineering in Madras Institute of Technology.
  • He served as the 11th President of INDIA.

  • During his term of Presidency he was known as the people's President.
  • He was also known as the MISSLE MAN OF INDIA as he sucessfully directed two projects ie PROJECT DEVIL and Project Valient.
  • On 27 July 2015 while delivering a speech in Indian Institute of Management, Shillong,he collapsed just after being 5 minutes into his levture on "Creating A Livable Planet Earth".
  • Kalam was declared dead due to Cardiac Arrest in 2015.
  • In February 2018, scientists from the Botanical Survey of India named a newly found plant species as Drypetes kalamii, in his honour.
